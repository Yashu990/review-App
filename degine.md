About This App – 
"Review me" is a cutting-edge platform crafted to empower businesses in enhancing their online presence and boosting sales through the strategic management of customer reviews. Initially a part of the Cloud Home dashboard, "Review me" has evolved into a standalone app, offering small business owners a streamlined approach to review management.

Key Features
•	Smart Feedback Requests: Effortlessly send review requests via email, encouraging customers to share their experiences with your business.
•	Custom QR Codes: Create personalized QR codes linked to your Google business listing, enabling customers to leave reviews with ease.
•	All in one Review Dashboard: Manage and organize all customer reviews from one easy-to-navigate platform, complete with filtering and sorting options to prioritize feedback effectively.
•	The 5-star Brand: Highlight your top reviews to bolster your online credibility and attract more customers.
•	Review Story: Delve into customer feedback with detailed reports and visual data representations to make informed, strategic business decisions.
•	Google Maps Integration: Showcase your most exceptional reviews directly on your Google Maps listing to draw in more clientele.

Why Choose "Review me"?
•	Boost Customer Trust: Positive reviews are pivotal in building credibility and trust, which are essential for attracting new customers.
•	Increase Customer Engagement: By frequently interacting with customers through review requests and responses, you can foster increased loyalty and engagement.
•	Maximize Efficiency: Automating the review management process saves time and helps keep your business organized and efficient.
•	Future-Proof Your Business: Regular updates and the introduction of new features ensure your business stays ahead in the realm of online reputation management.
Target Users
•	Small Businesses

•	Service Providers

5.	Analyse and Improve: Leverage detailed analytics to enhance your business’s performance.
